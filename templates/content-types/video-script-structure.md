## Standard Structure

### HOOK (First 3 Seconds)

**Purpose:** Stop the scroll. Grab attention instantly.

**Requirements:**
- Must be the single most compelling element (statistic, question, or bold claim)
- Visual and audio hook must work simultaneously
- Viewer decides to stay or leave in this window

**Hook Types:**

1. **Statistic Hook**
```
DIALOGUE: "73% of doctors now use AI."
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "73%" (large, centered, animated in)
B-ROLL: Hospital technology montage
MUSIC: Dramatic beat drop
```

2. **Question Hook**
```
DIALOGUE: "Is AI about to replace your doctor?"
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Is AI replacing doctors?"
B-ROLL: Close-up of AI interface with medical data
MUSIC: Suspenseful tone, builds tension
```

3. **Bold Claim Hook**
```
DIALOGUE: "AI just outperformed human doctors."
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "AI > Doctors?"
B-ROLL: Split screen -- AI system vs doctor examining patient
MUSIC: News-style dramatic sting
```

4. **Contradiction Hook**
```
DIALOGUE: "Everything you know about healthcare is about to change."
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "EVERYTHING changes."
B-ROLL: Fast montage of traditional vs modern medicine
MUSIC: Cinematic buildup
```

**Platform Adjustments:**
- **TikTok:** Hook in 1 second, not 3. Text must appear frame 1.
- **Instagram Reels:** Visual hook first, then dialogue.
- **YouTube:** Can use 3-5 seconds. Include channel branding if series.
- **Explainer:** Problem statement as hook. Can be 5 seconds.

---

### INTRO (10-15% of total length)

**Purpose:** Frame the topic, set expectations, tell viewer what they will learn.

**Components:**
1. **Topic Statement** (1-2 sentences) -- What this video covers
2. **Why Now** (1 sentence) -- Timeliness or urgency
3. **Value Promise** (1 sentence) -- What viewer gains by watching

**Example (5-minute YouTube):**
```
TIMESTAMP: 0:03-0:30

DIALOGUE:
"In the last 12 months, AI in healthcare went from experimental to essential.
Hospitals are deploying systems that diagnose faster, predict outcomes better,
and cost a fraction of traditional methods. By the end of this video, you'll
know the 3 biggest breakthroughs -- and how they affect you."

ON-SCREEN TEXT:
- "AI in Healthcare: 2026" (title card)
- "3 Breakthrough Applications" (preview)

B-ROLL: Modern hospital, AI dashboard screens, doctor reviewing data

MUSIC: Upbeat, forward-moving electronic (120 BPM)
```

### MAIN CONTENT -- Scene Format

**Each scene follows this format:**

```
=== SCENE [NUMBER]: [SCENE TITLE] ===
TIMESTAMP: [START] - [END]
DURATION: [X seconds]

--- DIALOGUE ---
[Exact words the speaker says. Timed to 120-150 words per minute.
Include pause markers (PAUSE) for emphasis or B-roll moments.]

--- ON-SCREEN TEXT ---
[Key phrases, statistics, or bullet points displayed on screen.
1-3 text elements per scene. Large, readable, branded fonts.]

Line 1: [text element]
Line 2: [text element]
Line 3: [text element]

--- B-ROLL ---
[Specific visual descriptions. Be concrete enough for a video editor
to source or shoot the footage.]

Shot 1: [description] ([duration])
Shot 2: [description] ([duration])
Shot 3: [description] ([duration])

--- MUSIC / SFX ---
[Mood, tempo, and any specific sound effects.]

Music: [description of mood/genre/tempo]
SFX: [any specific sound effects at timestamps]

--- TRANSITION ---
[How this scene connects to the next. Cut, fade, swipe, graphic wipe, etc.]
```

**Example Scene (5-minute YouTube):**
```
=== SCENE 2: AI Diagnostic Accuracy ===
TIMESTAMP: 1:30 - 2:30
DURATION: 60 seconds

--- DIALOGUE ---
"The first breakthrough is accuracy. A study in The Lancet tested an AI
system against 200 experienced radiologists. (PAUSE)

The AI detected cancerous lesions with 94.5% accuracy. The average
radiologist? 88%.

That 6.5% gap might sound small, but in a hospital processing 500
scans a day, it means 32 additional early catches. Per day. Every day.

And the AI never gets tired. It never has an off morning. It processes
its 500th scan with the same precision as its first."

--- ON-SCREEN TEXT ---
Line 1: "94.5% AI Accuracy" (animated counter from 0%)
Line 2: "vs 88% Human Average" (appears after counter)
Line 3: "+32 early catches per day" (emphasized with highlight)

--- B-ROLL ---
Shot 1: Radiologist examining X-rays on lightbox (5s)
Shot 2: AI interface highlighting lesion on scan (8s)
Shot 3: Split-screen comparison: AI vs human detection overlay (7s)
Shot 4: Time-lapse of hospital scan workflow, dawn to dusk (5s)
Shot 5: Close-up of scan results with AI confidence scores (5s)

--- MUSIC / SFX ---
Music: Continues upbeat electronic, slight intensity increase
SFX: Subtle "ping" when 94.5% counter hits target
SFX: Heartbeat sound effect during "32 additional early catches"

--- TRANSITION ---
Graphic wipe with data visualization element leading into Scene 3
```

### CTA (Call to Action)

**Purpose:** Drive a specific viewer action.

**Components:**
1. **Recap Trigger** (1 sentence) -- Brief callback to key value
2. **Specific Ask** (1 sentence) -- Exactly what to do
3. **Motivation** (1 sentence) -- Why do it now

**Platform CTAs:**

**YouTube:**
```
DIALOGUE: "If this opened your eyes to AI in healthcare, hit subscribe
and turn on notifications -- I'm covering AI breakthroughs every week.
Link to the full research is in the description."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "SUBSCRIBE" (animated button), "Link in description"
B-ROLL: End screen template with subscribe button and related video
```

**TikTok:**
```
DIALOGUE: "Follow for more AI insights."
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Follow for Part 2" (large, centered)
B-ROLL: Point-at-screen gesture, follow button highlight
```

**Instagram Reels:**
```
DIALOGUE: "Save this for later. Share it with someone in healthcare."
ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Save + Share" (with icons)
B-ROLL: Share button animation
```

**Explainer:**
```
DIALOGUE: "Download our free AI Healthcare Implementation Guide at
acmemed.com/guide. It covers everything we discussed today, plus
step-by-step instructions for your team."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "acmemed.com/guide" (prominent), "FREE Guide"
B-ROLL: Guide mockup, website landing page
```

---

### OUTRO (YouTube only, 5-10% of total length)

**Purpose:** End screen with related content and subscribe.

**Components:**
1. **Closing Line** -- Memorable sign-off
2. **End Screen Overlay** -- 20 seconds with subscribe + related video
3. **Channel Branding** -- Logo, tagline

**Example:**
```
TIMESTAMP: 4:40 - 5:00

DIALOGUE: "Thanks for watching. I'm [Speaker Name], and this is [Channel].
See you in the next one."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Channel name + logo

B-ROLL: End screen template (20s)
- Left: Related video thumbnail (clickable)
- Right: Subscribe button (clickable)
- Center: Channel logo

MUSIC: Fade out to channel theme
```

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### YouTube Optimization
- **End Screen:** Last 20 seconds reserved for clickable elements
- **Mid-Roll Break:** At ~8:00 for 10-minute videos (natural topic transition)
- **Chapter Timestamps:** Include in description for 3min+ videos
- **Pattern Interrupts:** New visual element every 30-60 seconds to maintain retention
- **Cards:** Reference related videos at relevant moments (1-2 per video)
- **Thumbnail Suggestion:** Include text overlay concept + visual concept

### TikTok Optimization
- **Hook:** Must land in 1 second (text on screen before dialogue starts)
- **Fast Cuts:** New visual every 2-3 seconds
- **Trending Audio:** Reference trending sounds or use original with hook
- **Loop Potential:** Last frame should connect thematically to first frame
- **Text-Heavy:** Assume 40% of viewers watch without sound
- **Vertical:** All visual directions assume 9:16 frame
- **Stitch/Duet Ready:** Open with a statement others can react to

### Instagram Reels Optimization
- **Captions Required:** Full caption overlay for sound-off viewing
- **Visual First:** Lead with the strongest visual, then add dialogue
- **Aesthetic Consistency:** Match brand color palette and visual style
- **Smooth Transitions:** Prefer fades and smooth cuts over hard cuts
- **Music:** Background mood music, lower energy than TikTok
- **Stories Cross-Post:** Script should work if reshared to Stories (vertical, brief)

### Explainer Video Optimization
- **Slower Pace:** 120 words/minute maximum (allow processing time)
- **Professional Graphics:** Direct motion graphics and animated diagram descriptions
- **Pause Points:** Include (PAUSE 2s) after complex data for viewer comprehension
- **Section Markers:** Clear visual transitions between topics
- **Branded Elements:** Consistent color palette, logo watermark, branded lower thirds
- **No Face Needed:** Script assumes voiceover unless speaker specified

## Quality Standards

### Hook Effectiveness
- [ ] Grabs attention in first 3 seconds (1s for TikTok)
- [ ] Uses one of the 4 hook types (Statistic, Question, Bold Claim, Contradiction)
- [ ] Visual and audio hook work simultaneously
- [ ] Would you stop scrolling for this? (honest assessment)

### Dialogue Quality
- [ ] Paced at 120-150 words per minute
- [ ] Conversational, not scripted-sounding (read aloud test)
- [ ] No more than 3 sentences before a visual change
- [ ] Includes pause markers for emphasis and B-roll moments
- [ ] Matches brand voice if brand profile provided

### Visual Direction
- [ ] B-roll described for every major point (no talking-head-only segments > 15s)
- [ ] On-screen text for every key statistic and takeaway
- [ ] Visual variety (no single shot type for more than 10 seconds)
- [ ] All B-roll descriptions specific enough for an editor to source

### Pacing & Structure
- [ ] Each scene has clear purpose (no filler)
- [ ] Transitions between scenes are smooth and logical
- [ ] Energy builds toward CTA (not flat throughout)
- [ ] Total dialogue word count matches target duration (+/- 5%)

### CTA Effectiveness
- [ ] Single, clear action (not multiple competing asks)
- [ ] Platform-appropriate (subscribe for YouTube, follow for TikTok)
- [ ] Motivated (reason to act now)
- [ ] Placed at natural conclusion point (not abrupt)

## SEO Section (YouTube Only)

### Title Optimization
- **Length:** 50-60 characters
- **Structure:** [Keyword] + [Curiosity/Benefit]
- **Examples:**
  - "AI in Healthcare: 3 Breakthroughs Changing Everything (2026)"
  - "How AI Diagnostics Outperform Doctors by 6.5% -- Here's Proof"
  - "The Future of Medicine: AI Trends Every Doctor Should Know"

```

In this video:

00:30 - [Scene 1 Title]
01:30 - [Scene 2 Title]
02:30 - [Scene 3 Title]
03:30 - [Scene 4 Title]

- [Link 1]
- [Link 2]

```

### Tags
- 10-15 relevant tags

- Include competitor video keywords (research what top results use)

## Common Pitfalls

**Do NOT:**
- Write dialogue that sounds like a blog post read aloud (too formal, too long sentences)
- Include B-roll directions that are too vague ("relevant footage" -- be specific)
- Create wall-of-text on-screen text (max 6-8 words per overlay)
- Forget sound-off viewers (TikTok/Instagram need text overlays for all key points)
- Use the same shot type for the entire video (variety keeps attention)
- End without a CTA (every video needs a clear next step)
- Ignore platform-specific pacing (TikTok 30s paced like YouTube 5min fails)
- Write for longer than the target duration (discipline on word count)

**DO:**
- Write dialogue the way someone talks, not the way someone writes
- Include specific B-roll descriptions an editor can execute
- Front-load value (hook + first scene deliver core insight)
- Use on-screen text to reinforce, not repeat, the dialogue
- Include music/SFX directions for mood setting
- Read the script aloud before finalizing (if you stumble, rewrite)
- Match pacing to platform norms (fast for TikTok, measured for Explainer)
- Include (PAUSE) markers where the speaker should breathe or let a point land
